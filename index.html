ABOUT US

Established in 2017, Team 360 Design Studio stands as a premier architectural and interior design firm based in Nagpur, Maharashtra and Vesu, Surat, Gujarat. Operating at the intersection of technical engineering precision and high-end artistic design, we deliver bespoke spatial solutions for an elite clientele. Over nearly a decade of growth, we have expanded from a boutique setup into a multidisciplinary practice known for uncompromising structural diagnostics and cinematic design aesthetics. We specialize in high-end residential bungalow designs, modern commercial office spaces, structural layout planning, and comprehensive turnkey interior solutions. From initial concept layouts and detailed 3D elevations to final on-site execution, we prioritize exceptional space planning, premium material selection, and luxury craftsmanship. Partner with Team 360 Design Studio to transform your blueprints into breathtaking, permanent realities.

Gaurav V. Dubewar

Founding Director
NICMAR Post Graduate JLL Mahindra Lifespaces

Gaurav brings a distinguished corporate background forged within India’s leading real estate and property development institutions, including JLL (Jones Lang LaSalle) and Mahindra Lifespaces. Throughout his tenure across these industry giants, he specialized in developer-level project metrics, commercial risk mitigation, and large-scale operational governance.

At Team 360 Studio, Gaurav translates this high-level enterprise intelligence into the specialized context of luxury residential and commercial interior design. By implementing institutional-grade dashboard tracking, rigorous procurement compliance, and precise financial forecasting, he ensures every project is executed with transparent financial discipline, optimal resource allocation, and absolute operational efficiency.

Rishav V. Bansal

Founding Director
NICMAR Post Graduate L&T Realty

Rishav’s engineering foundation was built within the high-stakes structural operations of L&T Realty (Larsen & Toubro), one of Asia’s premier infrastructure and construction conglomerates. Managing complex, large-scale projects at L&T instilled in him an uncompromising standard for engineering precision, strict material compliance, and rigorous timeline management.

At Team 360 Studio, Rishav heads technical execution and on-site engineering operations. He brings deep structural discipline, advanced procurement standards, and contractual schedule adherence to every residential and turnkey commercial fit-out. Under his technical leadership, Team 360 Studio guarantees builds that are not only aesthetically exceptional, but structurally unimpeachable and delivered precisely on schedule.
